Justin Feng

Hi, my name is Justin Feng, I'm a computer engineer with a minor in cybersecurity and mathematics. I love learning about new things and technologies as well as helping others learn and grow along with me. I have a ton of analog, digital and software experience loving all of it. I'm a hard worker who is always eager to make the best product I can for the people I'm working for. I'm a great communicator who works great either on a team or leading one.

Throughout high school I loved math and science and so engineering felt like the natural choice of major. Even in high school I had some experience in digital design due to a class about that exact topic. From then on, I knew I wanted to be an electrical engineer. But when I got to college, I had to take an intro python class, which opened me up to the world of software. After that I decided I wanted to learn both hardware and software, so my major became computer engineering where I could learn both sides of computers and technology. Since then, I have been exposed to tons of new subjects and subfields within computer engineering all of which I hope to one day get to know very well.

Other than academics, I also love hanging out with friends in my free time. I enjoy playing sports like soccer and volleyball as well as board/card games such as catan, ticket to ride, exploding kittens, chess and pandemic. I really love games that make you think ahead and use your brain which is why I loved engineering and computer science. They present a problem which makes me think to find the most efficient way to solve it.
